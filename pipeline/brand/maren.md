# MAREN — house brand for the beauty demo store

Demo house brand built from a de-branded blend of clean, skin-first prestige beauty.
Internal Spectrum demo store only. Name is swappable.

## Positioning
Considered essentials for skin-first beauty. Quiet luxury — fewer, better products.

## Voice
- Restrained, editorial, confident. No hype, no exclamation marks, no "revolutionary".
- Lowercase-leaning in UI display; sentence case in body copy.
- Sensory but precise. Lead with what it does and how it feels, not adjectives stacked.
- Honest about ingredients and finish. Never fabricate claims.

## Naming convention (functional / minimal)
- Drop proprietary marketing prefixes (Swipe, GetSet, ShineOn, VibePlump, etc.) and ®/™.
- Keep the functional descriptor: finish + format + category.
- Examples: "Serum Concealer", "Matte Powder Blush", "Weightless Lipstick", "Skin Tint SPF".

## Aesthetic (drives the Ritual theme restyle)
- Palette: warm stone neutrals (bone, oat, taupe, soft clay), near-black ink, one muted accent.
- Type: a fine high-contrast serif for display + a clean grotesque sans for UI/body.
- Layout: generous white space, calm grids, restrained motion.
- Photography: soft daylight, real skin texture, minimal props.

## Content rules (carry into all store copy)
- No em-dashes in rendered text. Use commas, periods, or middots.
- No numeric counters / "N of M" in UI.
- No fabricated scarcity; FOMO signals must map to real (synthetic-but-consistent) data.
